About Siyu Chen is an artist, maker and educator based in Shanghai, China. She draws, make things through different forms of co-working relationships, teaches, design tools for teaching. Since 2019, Siyu has worked as an assisting research fellow at Tongji University, focusing on educational research and design in digital fabrication at Fab Lab O. She holds a BFA in Painting from Rhode Island School of Design. She is an alumni of Fab Academy.
